FRAUD DETECTION IN FINTECH

Financial fraud is a major challenge in today’s digital economy. With the increasing use of online banking and digital payments, fraudsters are constantly developing new methods to exploit system weaknesses.

Common Types of Financial Fraud

  • Credit card fraud
  • Identity theft
  • Unauthorized transactions
  • Account takeover attacks

Traditional fraud detection systems rely on predefined rules, which are often unable to detect new or complex fraud patterns. This can lead to missed threats and increased financial risk for businesses and customers.

SentinAI uses machine learning to analyse large volumes of transaction data in real time. By identifying unusual behaviour, location changes, and suspicious spending patterns, the system can detect possible fraud instantly and take action before a payment is approved.
